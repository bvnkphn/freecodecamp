Thai Basil Pork Stir-Fry

Stir-Fried Basil with Minced Pork

Ingredients

  • 200g minced pork
  • 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  • 1 tbsp chopped chili
  • 1 handful holy basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar

Instructions

  1. Stir-fry garlic and chili in oil until fragrant.
  2. Add minced pork and stir until cooked.
  3. Season with oyster sauce, soy sauce, and sugar.
  4. Toss in basil leaves, stir quickly, and turn off the heat. Ready to serve!
